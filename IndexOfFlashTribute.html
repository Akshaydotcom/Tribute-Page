The superhero you see here is none other than the one and only Flash. For those of you who don't know him, (I am judging you so hard right now), lemme give you a brief insight. The Flash is one of main founders of Justice League, of the DC comics Universe, and his most notable powers include the ability to move/run at incredible speeds, along with a good healing factor, which keeps him alive and kicking at any point of the storyline. However the most underrated ability he possesses is the ability to think and predict the outcome of any event within an attosecond. An attosecond is the minute amount of time taken by light to move from one atom to another....Yeah, you read that right, from one atom to another.
